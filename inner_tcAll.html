Full Cycle UX for a continuous feature release

The project

As part of their offering, Thomas Cook and Ving provide an immersive mobile experience for their customers that includes: mobile payment, web views, to do lists, calendar features, search functionality, booking management, profile management and more.


I worked closely with both back-and-frontend developers (in Sweden and Ukraine) as the sole resource for a variety of needs - complex wireframes, detailed interactions, overall information architecture, A/B testing, stakeholder workshops, requirement analysis, and coaching junior UX staff. I've helped improve collaboration between UX and developer teams to help both sides take ownership of the end product.


You can download the app on the App Store and Google Play.

Challenges

The Ving app is used by tens of thousands of people on a daily basis, so every change has to be well thought out and thorough. We've worked across several teams in different countries which called for tight collaboration and making sure everyone's involved in the decisions made.
