Next generation financial business platform

The project

* This was a project under a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), so below is a very general description. *
Danske Bank launched a moonshot project to build a next-generation financial platform. It enables corporate customers and institutions to get an overview of their financial situation as well as to simplify day-to-day workings when it comes to financial services. This high-profile project uses cutting-edge frontend technologies and modern UX principles to make complex user-flows and data visualisations understandable, user-friendly and approachable.

You can find more about the project by reading the official announcement from Danske Bank or watch the video demo.

The financial platform is made up of an intricate array of moving parts that all have to deliver value in a uniform, omnichannel way. We conducted research, built wireframes, information architecture, requirement analysis, and went through high-paced UX design cycles to deliver a variety of solutions, e.g. Payments, Liquidity, Cash Flow Forecasting, and much more.

Challenges

The platform is designed to cater to a wide variety of clients, ranging from small business owners to corporates and institutions. This requirement mandated that every solution can support different expectations and workflows, which was an important focus for the team. We also had to ensure that the different solutions are not siloed, but instead work as an ecosystem aware of different moving parts.
