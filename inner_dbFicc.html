Complex website UX overhaul

The project

Danske Bank decided to do a complete overhaul of an important client-facing service - a place where they share financial/market data. I was the responsible UX designer, serving as the meeting ground between development and business goals. The project involved 12 business-side stakeholders, 3 developers, 1 User Experience specialist, across three offices in Denmark, Latvia and India.

Challenges

Financial research websites contain layers of pages and tens of thousands of articles. There's a need for a finely tuned navigation system that enables fast, keyword-based search/filtering while at the same time staying almost completely out of sight.


As the basis of the website overhaul, we had to use the existing research depository which was plagued both with indecipherable design and legal conundrums.
