The project

PAC Method was a small startup based in Croatia which aimed to develop a new type of file sharing, similar to Dropbox and Box but with a different approach to privacy and security. When I came on board, the MVP was fully functional in terms of features – however, no thought was given to UX principles which resulted in a difficult and complex application onboarding – many users did not know how to use the application without extensive instructions.

My role:

Research

Ideation

Prototyping

User Testing


I conducted a series of interview with target demographics (lawyers, government officials, police force, etc) and a competitor analysis. Based on that information, we created several personas and presented their onboarding experience through user journeys and task models which resulted in the identification of several critical onboarding drop-off points. Upon delivery of the findings, the startup decided to redesign the onboarding process in line with the UX recommendations.
