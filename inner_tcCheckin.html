VING Check-In feature

The project

To offer our customers the best possible service, Ving decided to offer an in-app flight check-in feature so that everything related to upcoming trips lives in one place. This called for extensive conceptualization, user testing, and serious creativity and negotiation to work with the checkin data providers API.


You can download the app on the App Store and Google Play.

Challenges

The flight checkin data was provided by an external partner whose API's were highly inefficient and difficult to work with. To get around this, we went through an intense series of ideation and conceptualization to both get the best service available and to cut on costs and time needed to implement changes on the data-provider side.
